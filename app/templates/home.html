Track Your Spending Regularly

Keep a close eye on where your money goes. Small, repeated expenses often add up. Use a simple budget or app to track daily expenses

Create a Monthly Budget

Plan your income allocation — for needs, wants, savings, and investments. Try the 50/30/20 rule

Build an Emergency Fund

Whether it's buying a home, traveling, or early retirement — set specific, measurable, and time-bound goals. Your saving and investment strategies should align with them

Set Financial Goals

Start by saving at least 3–6 months’ worth of your monthly expenses. This protects you from unexpected events like job loss or medical emergencies
